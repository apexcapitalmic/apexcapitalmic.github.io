About Us

Apex Capital Management Limited is a licensed mortgage administrator for Apex Mortgage Investment Corporation serving borrowers and investors across GTA.

We provide flexible private mortgage lending solutions backed by real estate, offering an alternative to traditional financing for borrowers and attractive risk-adjusted returns for investors.

What We Do

Private Mortgage Lending

Flexible short-term mortgage solutions for residential and commercial properties, with efficient underwriting and fast funding.

Investment Opportunities

Access to a diversified portfolio of mortgage investments secured by Ontario real estate, offering competitive yields.

Broker Partnerships

Working closely with mortgage brokers to deliver timely financing solutions for their clients.
